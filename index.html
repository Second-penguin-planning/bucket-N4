How to Use

1. Enter your name and press Start.

2. Look at the kanji and try to remember the reading and meaning.

3. Press "Show Answer" to check.

4. Choose your answer:

  • Easy → you knew it
  • OK → almost correct
  • Hard → difficult
  • Don't Know → you didn't know

Why this method?

This app uses the Bucket Learning Method.

Words you don't know appear more often, and words you know appear less often.

This is based on Spaced Repetition, a scientifically proven method to remember vocabulary longer.
